About me

Hello! I'm Devon Barrett, a dedicated web developer and former STEM teacher with a passion for empowering individuals through education and technology. With a background in computer science from Tennessee Technological University, I've utilized my skills to teach computer science to 5th - 8th-grade students at Liberty Collegiate Academy, focusing on engaging projects that enhance learning and creativity.

With a rich background in full-stack web development, I excel at crafting robust and scalable applications that enhance user experiences and business operations. My proficiency with industry-standard web development platforms empowers me to deliver sophisticated and responsive solutions. I am dedicated to utilizing my comprehensive programming skills to contribute to significant projects that drive innovation and extend access to cutting-edge technical skills in underserved communities.

I am particularly interested in starting up a learning center aimed at teaching essential technical skills to marginalized groups, thus providing them with more opportunities to pursue their dreams. My journey is documented on my LinkedIn profile, where you can connect with me and learn more about my work and projects.
